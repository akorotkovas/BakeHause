About

The Bakehouse is a bakery and eatery in the heart of Dublin city, which is located beside the ha'penny bridge. We want to remind Dublin of the traditional foods & breads our parents and grandparents were raised on, and how comforting and satisfying these are!

We offer great traditional Irish baked goods and foods. Everything is freshly & lovingly prepared in house every day. Whether its enjoying our freshly ground coffee or loose leaf tea with a pastry, or a hearty pie with a glass of wine – there is something for any time of day & all tastes. All of our breads and cakes are available to enjoy stitting in or to take away. Among our traditional favourites are turnover, batch, brown soda bread, coffee slices & angel cakes! You can contact us if you have specific requirments for parties or events.

Food Menu

Our flavoursome food served with our freshly baked bread is a combination that is hard to beat. Each dish provides you with yummy flavours & satisfying comfort!
